About Us

Comfort You Can Trust

Shri Naina Enterprises is a trusted mattress and pillow company based in Haroli, Una, Himachal Pradesh. Owned and operated by Sharad and Naveen, we are proudly powered by Spenta Mattress — a name known for premium quality sleep products.

Our focus is simple: bring genuine comfort, real quality, and honest service to every home. From soft foam pillows to orthopedic and spring mattresses, each product is chosen with care so you get durable, dependable comfort at a fair price.

  • Friendly service and honest pricing
